# Game World

Serpentic uses a **circular dynamic border** that grows and shrinks based on the number of active players.

## Arena Capacity

- **Maximum players per arena**: **40**
- Once the arena reaches 40 players, new players will be placed in a new arena (instance/shard).
- This limit ensures good performance and fair gameplay density.

## Border Radius

- **Base Radius**: 2400 units (1 player)
- **Growth**: +680 units per additional player
- **Maximum Radius**: 6800 units
- **Minimum Radius**: 2400 units

**Examples:**
- 1 player   → 2400 units radius
- 10 players → 9200 units radius
- 20 players → 16000 units radius (capped at max)
- 40 players → 6800 units radius (maximum size)

The playable area is defined by this glowing circular border, centered at (0, 0).

## Dynamic Border Behavior

When a player joins or leaves the arena:

- The border radius **smoothly expands** or **shrinks**.
- **Expand Speed**: 520 units/sec
- **Shrink Speed**: 380 units/sec (intentionally slow)

The animation is clearly visible and usually takes **6–12 seconds**, giving players near the border enough time to move to safety.

### Border Collision & Overhang Rules

- **Head collision**: If your **snake head** touches or crosses the border, you die instantly.
- **Body overhang**: If the border shrinks and part of your body ends up outside:
  - You **do not lose any segments**.
  - Your snake can temporarily overhang outside the border.
  - You can still move and control your snake normally.
  - Once you move fully back inside, everything continues as normal.
- Players near the shrinking border get a strong visual warning (flashing red border + sound) and extra time to react.

### When the Border Shrinks
- Reward Orbs near the border are **gently pushed inward**.
- Food (grow items) that end up outside the new radius are **immediately deleted**.

## Spawning Rules

### Food (Grow Items)
- Small glowing pieces that make your snake longer when eaten.
- **Spawn rate**: Player-dependent
  - Base: 1 food every 3 seconds
  - Gets faster with more players (+0.4 sec reduction per additional player)
- Only spawns **inside the current border radius**
- Never spawns within 350 units of any player
- Maximum food on map: 130–160

### Reward Orbs
- Larger, high-value orbs.
- Same spawning rules and border restrictions as food.

## Technical Details

- Soft glowing circular border.
- Hard collision only applies to the snake’s head.
- Border radius changes are synchronized in real-time.
- Temporary overhang is fully allowed.
- Server automatically creates new arenas when player count exceeds 40.
