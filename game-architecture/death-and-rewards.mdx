# Death & Rewards

Understanding death, orb drops, and the reward system is essential to mastering Serpentic.

## Death Mechanics

When your snake's **head** collides with another player's body or the world border, you die.

### What Happens on Death:

- Your entire snake body **breaks apart into Reward Orbs**.
- These orbs are dropped on the map and can be collected by any surviving player.
- Collecting your dropped orbs gives the other players extra balance.
- You lose the **entry fee** you paid to join the current arena session.
- Your **permanent PlayerBalance** (from previous successful cashouts) remains safe.

You do **not** lose balance earned during the session if you successfully cash out before dying.

## Rewards & Economy

### Successful Exit (Cashout)
- If you exit the arena safely (using the E key) and then cash out:
  - You keep **all** the balance you earned during the session (initial entry fee + rewards from orbs and food).
  - The balance is transferred to your connected wallet.

### Death Penalty
- You lose only the **entry fee** of the current session.
- Any orbs you collected during the session that are still on your snake are dropped and become loot for others.

### Orb Drops
- On death, your body turns into multiple collectible Reward Orbs.
- Bigger snakes = more orbs dropped.
- These orbs give a significant balance boost to whoever picks them up.

## Future Progression Systems

The following features are planned for future updates:

### Loot Boxes (Chests)
- NFT holders will be able to open special **Loot Boxes**.
- Better NFTs = better base rarity of loot boxes.
- Loot boxes can contain balance, cosmetic items, boosts, and more.

### XP & Upgrade System
- You earn **XP** based on time spent in-game and your performance.
- XP is used to **upgrade** your Loot Boxes.
- Higher tier boxes have significantly better rewards and drop rates.
- Full details about XP rates, upgrade costs, and loot tables will be available in the docs once implemented.

|-----------------------------|--------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Die in arena                | Entry fee of current session   | Previous cashouts stay safe              |
| Successful cashout          | Nothing                        | Full session earnings + entry fee back   |
| Other player dies           | -                              | Opportunity to collect their dropped orbs|

## Tips

- Cash out regularly if you have a good run — don’t risk everything.
- Dying near the center gives others more time to collect your orbs.
- Hunting big snakes near death can be very profitable.
- Play smart and know when to exit.
