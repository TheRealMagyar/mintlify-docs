## Welcome

Serpentic is a real-time multiplayer snake arena game built on the SUI blockchain. Grow your snake, hunt Reward Orbs, and cash out your earnings before you die. Every session is a live economic game — your balance is real, your risk is real, and your cashout lands on-chain.

This documentation covers everything: how to play, how the game systems work under the hood, how to integrate with the public API, and how the backend infrastructure is structured.
