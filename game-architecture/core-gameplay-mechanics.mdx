# Core Gameplay Mechanics

Serpentic is a fast-paced multiplayer snake arena game where you grow your snake, collect rewards, and survive against other players.

## Objective

Grow the longest snake possible, collect valuable **Reward Orbs**, and cash out your earnings before getting eliminated.  
The more you eat and the longer you survive, the higher your balance becomes.

## Basic Mechanics

- Your snake moves **automatically forward** at all times.
- You control only the **direction** of the head.
- Eating **Food** makes your snake longer and stronger.
- Eating **Reward Orbs** gives you in-game balance (that can be withdrawn).

## Controls Recap

- **Arrow Keys** — Turn left / right  
- **Space** or **Up Arrow** — Boost (much faster movement)  
- **Mouse Movement** — Alternative steering  
- **E** — Hold 4 seconds to cash out (after exiting arena)

## Growth & Eating

- **Food** (small glowing dots):  
  - Increases snake length  
  - Common and constantly spawning  
- **Reward Orbs** (larger, brighter):  
  - Give significant balance reward  
  - Rarer, higher value  
- The longer your snake, the more dangerous and slower you become (without boost).

## Combat & Elimination

- You die instantly if your **head** collides with:
  - Another player’s body
  - The circular world border
- If another player’s head hits **your body**, they die and you get a small reward bonus.
- When a player dies, they drop some of their collected orbs for others to pick up.

## Arena & Survival

- Maximum **40 players** per arena.
- The circular border dynamically shrinks or expands based on player count.
- You can temporarily overhang the border with your body (no penalty), but your head cannot touch it.
- Last player standing or highest earnings wins the session.

## Progression & Economy

- Everything you collect adds to your **PlayerBalance**.
- You can safely exit the arena and cash out your balance to your wallet at any time (using E).
- Dying does **not** remove your balance — only the current session progress is lost.

## Core Loop

1. Join an arena
2. Eat food to grow quickly
3. Hunt Reward Orbs while avoiding bigger snakes
4. Use boost strategically to escape or chase
5. Cash out your earnings when ready
6. Repeat

## Advanced Tips

- Bigger snakes are slower — use boost wisely.
- Trap smaller players by circling them.
- Stay near the center when the border is shrinking.
- High risk = high reward near crowded areas.
