*Cashout flow — Press and hold E for 4 seconds to withdraw your balance*

The Cashout System allows players to withdraw their in-game earnings (initial deposit + rewards from orbs) back to their wallet at any time.

## How Cashout Works

1. **Press & Hold E** — While in the arena, press and hold the **E** key for **4 seconds**.
2. **No Control During Hold** — Your snake becomes uncontrollable while holding E (this prevents accidental or exploitative cashouts).
3. **Validation** — The system checks:
   - You are not in an active game session
   - Death cooldown has passed (if you died recently)
4. **Withdrawal** — Your entire `PlayerBalance` is transferred back to your connected wallet.

## Safety Mechanisms

- **Session Lock**: You cannot cash out while inside an active arena session.
- **Death Cooldown**: After dying, you must wait 60 seconds before cashing out.
- **Two-Step Exit**: 
  - First `exit_session()` (called after successful E hold)
  - Then `cash_out()` transaction
- All gas fees are paid by the player.

## Technical Details

- Powered by the `PlayerBalance` smart contract on Sui.
- Uses owned objects — your balance is always under your control.
- All transactions (exit + cashout) are initiated by the player.
- Backend only validates and triggers; never pays gas.

## When Can You Cash Out?

| Situation                    | Can Cash Out? | Reason |
|-----------------------------|---------------|--------|
| Alive + in arena            | No            | Active session |
| Died recently               | No            | 60s cooldown |
| Successfully exited arena   | Yes           | Ready |
| No active session           | Yes           | Immediate |
