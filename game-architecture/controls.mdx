# Game Controls

Learn how to control your snake effectively in Serpentic.

## Movement

- **Arrow Keys** — Primary controls
  - **Left Arrow** ← : Turn left
  - **Right Arrow** → : Turn right
  - **Up Arrow** ↑ : **Boost** (same as Space)

- **Mouse Movement** — Alternative control
  - Move your mouse to steer the snake in that direction (mouse follows / relative steering)

Your snake moves forward automatically. You only need to change its direction.

## Boost

**Space** or **Up Arrow** ↑

- Hold to activate boost
- Your snake moves significantly faster while boosting
- Boosting consumes energy (shown on the boost bar)
- Perfect for catching orbs, escaping, or quick maneuvers

You can also press **any mouse button** while holding boost.

## Cashout

**E Key**

- Press and hold **E** for **4 seconds** to withdraw your entire balance
- Your snake becomes uncontrollable during the hold (security feature)
- Only works after you have exited the arena session
- 60-second cooldown after death

## Control Summary

| Action              | Controls                              | Description |
|---------------------|---------------------------------------|-----------|
| Turn Left           | **Left Arrow** ←                      | Rotate snake left |
| Turn Right          | **Right Arrow** →                     | Rotate snake right |
| Boost               | **Space** or **Up Arrow** ↑           | Hold to move faster |
| Boost (alternative) | **Any Mouse Button**                  | Hold while boosting |
| Steer               | **Mouse Movement**                    | Point mouse to change direction |
| Cash Out            | **E** (hold 4 seconds)                | Withdraw balance to wallet |

## Tips

- You can combine mouse steering with arrow keys freely.
- Holding **Up Arrow** works the same as holding **Space** for boosting.
- Use boost strategically — it drains energy over time.
- Always exit the arena session before holding **E** to cash out.
- Mouse control is very responsive and recommended for precise movements.
