*Serpentic Genesis Pass — Limited Edition of 555 identical pieces*

Genesis NFTs are the original and most prestigious limited edition collection in Serpentic. Only **555** will ever exist, making them the rarest and most exclusive NFTs in the project.

These Genesis Passes reward early supporters with meaningful long-term utility and mark them as core members of the Serpentic community.

## What are Genesis NFTs?

The **Genesis Pass** is a limited edition NFT collection (555 pieces) that represents the earliest supporters of Serpentic — the decentralized, SUI-based slither.io-style game.

Unlike traditional 1/1 NFTs, **all Genesis Passes look exactly the same**. They function as a prestigious membership pass rather than unique artworks.
